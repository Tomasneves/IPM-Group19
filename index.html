Stage 1

Project Proposal, 30 of Setember, 2022

For this project we propose creating an app that allows roommates, families or any group of people doing grocery shopping together to cooperate in the simple task of shopping. The goal is that if someone needs something at home they add it to the common shopping list and when anyone from the group goes to the grocery store, buys the items missing.
